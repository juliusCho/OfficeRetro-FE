Office Retro

Office Retro is a web application for keeping records of your work and achievement to help your career to improve by having self-retrospection.

Office Retro focuses on 4 key retrospections in your office work life:
  1. What I have done
  2. What hardships & struggles I have overcome
  3. What contributions I have made to my team/company
  4. What feedback I have received from my colleagues
